Via Belgica Route

The map above is part of our student project showcasing the main cities along the Via Belgica route, an ancient Roman road that spans several historically significant regions. As you trace the route, you'll find a variety of attractions, from ancient ruins to picturesque landscapes, offering a glimpse into the rich history and culture that characterize this historic pathway.

We've also included short reviews from fellow students who have completed portions of the route. Their insights and personal experiences provide a unique perspective on the journey, highlighting must-see spots and offering practical tips.

Our goal is to create an engaging and informative guide that encourages others to explore and appreciate the Via Belgica.
