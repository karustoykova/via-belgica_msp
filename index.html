Via Belgica

The "Via Belgica" project delves into tracing the ancient Roman route known as the "Belgian Way," connecting Roman Cologne (Colonia Claudia Ara Agrippinensis) to Boulogne-sur-Mer (Gesoriacum) on the French coast. Passing through Roman Maastricht (Traiectum ad Mosam), nestled between Atuatuca Tungrorum (Tongeren) and Coriovallum (Heerlen), this road exemplifies Roman engineering, considering natural features such as rivers and hills, alongside strategic settlements and monuments.
